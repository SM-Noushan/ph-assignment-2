To Get Lost

Bangladesh is blessed with a lot of beautiful mountains in the world. The mountains make the overall natural landscape outstanding. Here are some of the most beautiful peaks in Bangladesh that you should not miss:

  • Keokradong is the highest peak in Bangladesh, standing at an elevation of 3,863 feet. The trek to the top of the peak is challenging but rewarding, with breathtaking views of the surrounding hills and valleys.
  • Saka Haphong is the second-highest peak in Bangladesh, standing at an elevation of 3,415 feet. The trek to the top of the peak is relatively easy and offers stunning views of the surrounding hills and valleys.
